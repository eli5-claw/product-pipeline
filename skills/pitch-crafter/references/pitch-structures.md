# Pitch Deck Templates

## 10-Slide Classic (Sequoia Format)

1. **Company Purpose** — One-liner + visual
2. **Problem** — Pain point, market gap
3. **Solution** — Product demo/screenshot
4. **Why Now?** — Timing, market shifts
5. **Market Size** — TAM/SAM/SOM
6. **Competition** — Landscape + differentiation
7. **Product** — Features, roadmap
8. **Business Model** — How you make money
9. **Team** — Why you specifically
10. **Financials/Ask** — Traction, raise, use of funds

## 12-Slide Variation

Add:
- **Traction** (separate slide)
- **Go-to-Market** (distribution strategy)

## Narrative Structures

### The Hero's Journey
User as hero, problem as villain, product as weapon.

### Before/After
Pain of current state → vision of better future → bridge (your product).

### The Insight
Contrarian take → evidence → opportunity → execution.
