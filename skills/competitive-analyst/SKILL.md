# Competitive Analyst

Map the battlefield. Find the gaps. Own the positioning.

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Market Mapping
- **TAM/SAM/SOM**: Total addressable, serviceable, obtainable market
- **Market maturity**: Emerging, growing, mature, declining
- **Key players**: Incumbents, challengers, niche players

### 2. Competitor Profiling
For each key competitor:
- **Product**: Features, UX, quality
- **Pricing**: Model, tiers, value proposition
- **Distribution**: Channels, partnerships, sales motion
- **Positioning**: Messaging, target audience, brand
- **Strengths**: What they do well
- **Weaknesses**: Gaps to exploit

### 3. Positioning Analysis
- **Perceptual maps**: Price vs. quality, features vs. simplicity
- **White space**: Underserved segments or needs
- **Differentiation**: Unique value proposition

### 4. Trend Analysis
- **Market trends**: Growth drivers, headwinds
- **Technology shifts**: Disruptors, enablers
- **Regulatory**: Compliance requirements, barriers
