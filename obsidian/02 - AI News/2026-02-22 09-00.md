## 2. Nvidia Nears $30 Billion Investment in OpenAI

Nvidia is reportedly close to finalizing a **$30 billion investment** in OpenAI as part of a massive funding round. This would value the ChatGPT maker at approximately **$730-830 billion**, marking one of the largest private capital raises on record.

Notably, the deal structure involves Nvidia investing for equity without a commensurate commitment from OpenAI to purchase Nvidia chips—a departure from typical supplier-customer arrangements. SoftBank and Amazon are also expected to participate in the round.

This investment replaces an earlier unfinished $100 billion long-term commitment between the two companies.

## 3. AI Discovers 25 New Magnetic Materials, Reducing Rare Earth Dependence

Researchers at the **University of New Hampshire** have used AI to accelerate the discovery of advanced magnetic materials, identifying **25 previously unknown compounds** capable of maintaining magnetism at high temperatures.

The team created the **Northeast Materials Database**—a searchable resource containing **67,573 magnetic compounds**. The AI system reads scientific papers, extracts experimental data, and predicts magnetic properties, dramatically accelerating materials discovery.

This breakthrough could reduce dependence on costly, imported rare earth elements essential for electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, smartphones, and medical devices.

*Source: ScienceDaily, Nature Communications*
