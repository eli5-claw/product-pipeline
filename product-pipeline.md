# Eli5DeFi Product Pipeline

## Research Sources
- Product Hunt (daily launches)
- GitHub Trending (repos, stars)
- Crypto Twitter/X (viral projects)
- DeFiLlama (protocol launches)
- Dune Analytics (dashboards)
- Reddit (r/webdev, r/cryptocurrency, r/ai)
- Hacker News (Show HN)
- AppSumo/Gumroad (digital products)

## Scoring Criteria
| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Market Size | 25% | TAM/SAM/SOM |
| Trend Velocity | 20% | Growth rate, momentum |
| Technical Feasibility | 20% | Can we build it? |
| Monetization | 20% | Clear revenue path |
| Competition | 15% | Blue ocean vs red ocean |

## Pipeline Stages
1. **Discovery** — Raw research findings
2. **Evaluation** — Scored, ranked by potential
3. **Design** — Specs, wireframes, architecture
4. **Build** — Active development
5. **Launch** — Deployed, marketed
6. **Scale** — Optimize, expand
