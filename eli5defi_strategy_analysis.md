## 6. Content Strategy Framework for Micro-Product Launches

### A. Pre-Launch: Education & Awareness Phase (2-3 weeks)

**Objective**: Prime the audience with relevant educational content

| Week | Content Type | Example |
|------|--------------|---------|
| 1 | Problem-aware thread | "The biggest problem with [current solution] that nobody talks about" |
| 2 | Educational deep-dive | "How [underlying technology] actually works (ELI5)" |
| 3 | Teaser/Build-in-public | "Been working on something to solve [problem]..." |

**Format**: Threads (8-12 tweets)
**CTA**: "What problems do YOU face with [current solutions]? Reply below"

## Conclusion

Eli5DeFi's content strategy succeeds because it **respects the audience's intelligence while removing complexity barriers**. For micro-product launches, this means:

1. **Educate before selling** - The product should feel like a natural extension of the educational content
2. **Simplicity is the differentiator** - If it can't be explained in an ELI5 thread, it's too complex
3. **Community-first** - Build with the audience, not for them
4. **Authenticity over hype** - The audience trusts Eli5DeFi because they don't oversell

The ideal micro-product for this audience is one that **saves them time, reduces complexity, or helps them make better decisions** in DeFi.
