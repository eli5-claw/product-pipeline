### The Experience

#### Unboxing
Each box contains:
- 5 mystery flavor packets
- Flavor reveal card (scratch-off)
- Collectible flavor card with AI-generated backstory
- Sticker of the "Flavor Mascot"
- Voting card (rate the flavor)

4. **The card** - Scratch off to confirm
